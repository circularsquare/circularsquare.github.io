flight map

This map shows estimated passengers traffic on every commercial route. Thicker and brighter lines have more traffic. The estimate assumes all flights are all 80% full. Cargo carriers and private jets are filtered out.

data

Flights come from Flightradar24, which tracks planes worldwide by fusing ADS-B, radar, satellite, and airline schedules. We pull every commercial departure over two full days: Friday and Saturday, 15-16 May 2026 and scale up to a year. Only two days are pulled because it's expensive. FR24 data has gaps for several countries, including Uzbekistan and Kuwait, and these were manually filled with data from flightsfrom.com.

The busiest routes are shown on the right side of the screen. When ranking by "unexpected", it shows routes that are unexpectedly busy relative to the traffic at either endpoint and the distance between them.
