About

This map shows TfL rail / London Tube trips taken on a typical weekday. The size of a train's bubble is proportional to the number of riders on it.

TfL publishes the NUMBAT dataset: 15-minute origin-destination ridership across the whole rail network, plus per-link train frequencies. Trains are derived from timetables.

Station coordinates from NUMBAT; track centrelines from OpenStreetMap contributors (ODbL). Map built with MapLibre GL and CARTO basemap tiles.
