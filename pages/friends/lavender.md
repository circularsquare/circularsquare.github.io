lavender is a plant with light purple flowers that grows in coastal [nachria](/places/nachria) and [shonei](/places/shonei). the flowers are very fragrant, and yield a sweet smelling oil. dried flowers are sometimes used in food or tea. the stems are resilient, growing woody over time. 

lavender is like a bird, thin and predatory. she likes wine and other flowers. sometimes she claims to be a boy, but no one believes her. 

lavender is a [community](/thoughts/community) organizer. every once in a while she and sage plan a community brunch and i get to meet fun people!

lavender is very smart, on a similar wavelength as [chrysanthemum](/friends/chrysanthemum). they have talked about *hegel*. lavender also has many interesting opinions regarding community and politics and the future.
