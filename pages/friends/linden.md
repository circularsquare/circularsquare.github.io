linden is a tree native to [melissa](/places/melissa), especially common in the city streets of [lindor](/places/lindor). it has plentiful sweet yellow flowers and sap, popular with aphids and [bee](/friends/bee). its wood is soft and light, used in sculptures and instruments. 

i knew linden at school. she used to carve wood at the workshop on the highest tower of the school. she also played guitar there, sometimes. she inspired me to learn how to play too.

nowadays she makes contraptions out of wood. so cool...
