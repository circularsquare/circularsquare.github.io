hi!

[this game](/circles) is heavily inspired by the quizzes at [hugequiz](https://hugequiz.com/) and [JetPunk](https://www.jetpunk.com/).

the city data is from [GeoNames](https://www.geonames.org/). the cities in this database are not disjoint! for instance, both new york city and manhattan are in this data. so a lot of population is double counted.
